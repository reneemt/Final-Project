About Me

Hey there! I'm Renée, an artist from San Jose, CA who loves playing with color, texture, and vibe. My digital art primarily focuses on people and poses. I try various tricks with color and rendering but my confidence is in my lineworks and poses.

My Inspiration

I’m a big fan of various artists like @naomyKWC, whose style and animations have inspired the content I'm shifting to create, and I am inspired by the character design of artists like @azi_makes_art. I want to get better at making characters and stories through practice in my free time.

How I Added Works

The categories in my gallery reflect contexts that I have created for:

  • Michigan in Color: The section in the Michigan Daily that I would draw for.
  • Studies: Includes learning pieces, exercises, and growth snapshots.
  • Free for All: Where I let loose and create with no rules and reason.

This site is part of my final project and my way of saying “look what I’ve made!” I hope you enjoy scrolling through and clicking around. Every piece has a little story behind it—even the messy ones.
