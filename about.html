About Me

Hey there! I'm an artist who loves playing with color, texture, and vibe. My digital art explores everything from Michigan landscapes to chaotic sketches and creative experiments that don’t fit any box.

The categories in my gallery reflect the different moods and modes I work in: Michigan in Color celebrates my home state in vibrant tones; Studies includes learning pieces, exercises, and growth snapshots; and Free for All is where I let loose and create with no rules.

This site is part of my final project and my way of saying “look what I’ve made!” I hope you enjoy scrolling through and clicking around. Every piece has a little story behind it—even the messy ones.
